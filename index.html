Steve Wozniak

Most famous for being the cofounder of Apple Inc., he was also the designer of the first personal computer, the Apple I, 45 years ago, in 1975.

As Daniel Kottke, an early Apple employee, aptly put it, "Between Woz and Jobs, Woz was the innovator, the inventor. Steve Jobs was the marketing person."

Although he was considered naive and an un-driven person, I still respect the guy and pay tribute to him here, for making his mark on the world.

You can read more about Steve Wozniak on his Wikipedia entry.
